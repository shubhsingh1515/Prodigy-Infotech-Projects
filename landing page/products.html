Our Products are :

Lead

A heavy metal often used in batteries due to its high density and electrical conductivity.

Battery Plate

A component in a battery where chemical reactions occur to generate and store electrical energy.

Lead oxide

A compound of lead and oxygen commonly found in the positive plates of lead-acid batteries.

Battery Scrap

Discarded or used batteries that may contain valuable materials like lead and are typically recycled to recover those materials.
